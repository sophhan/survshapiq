1. actg
- source: the mlr3proba R package
- publication: A controlled trial of two nucleoside analogues plus indinavir in persons with human immunodeficiency virus infection and CD4 cell counts of 200 per cubic millimeter or less. The New England Journal of Medicine, 1997. https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJM199709113371101
- n=1151, p=4
- nice features and interactions
- chaotic curves, nice number of terms


2. Pbc3
- source: the pec R package
- publication: Cyclosporin a treatment in primary biliary cirrhosis: Results of a long-term placebo controlled trial. Gastroenterology, 1993. https://doi.org/10.1016/0016-5085(93)90422-9
- n=349, p=7
- interesting features (lab tests)
- treatment binary feature
- many terms, attr > interactions


3. GRACE
- source: the mlr3proba R package
- publication: Global Registry of Acute Coronary Events (GRACE) hospital discharge risk score accurately predicts long-term mortality post acute coronary syndrome. American Heart Journal, 2007. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ahj.2006.10.004
- n=1000, p=3
- too simple, not interesting
